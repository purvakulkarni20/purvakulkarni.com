Context

Translating client business goals into shipped enterprise software.

Three years embedded in one of the largest Human Capital Management platforms in the world, I led implementation consulting engagements end to end. The work was simple to describe and difficult to do well. Take a client's organizational reality, structure it into a working configuration, and steer it through engineering until it went live and stayed live.

Engagements spanned discovery, scoping, stakeholder advisory, M&A integration, and post-launch adoption. Most ran concurrently. Every client had a different version of the same problem. How do you get your operations onto a system that does not yet know how you work?

Selected Engagements

Five things that defined the work.

  1. Led end-to-end client engagements for 40+ enterprise SaaS implementations simultaneously, owning discovery, scoping, stakeholder alignment, UAT, data migration, and go-live. Achieved 100% on-time delivery across all client accounts.

  2. Translated complex client business requirements into structured Customer Requirement Guides and configuration specifications, serving as the primary advisor between executive stakeholders and engineering. Reduced configuration errors by 25% and accelerated client time-to-adoption.

  3. Drove cross-functional collaboration with integration engineers, project managers, and product teams to triage feature gaps, prioritize enhancement requests, and resolve testing discrepancies. Improved issue resolution speed by 30% and shaped product roadmap based on direct client feedback.

  4. Led M&A integration work, migrating 4,000+ Human Capital records via API during active client acquisitions while owning data validation and conversion end to end. Ensured zero operational disruption at go-live and mitigated data integrity risks across legacy system transitions.

  5. Advised HR leadership across 3 long-cycle enterprise implementations on adoption strategy and organizational change management, aligning platform capabilities to business goals. Reduced post-launch support escalations by 15%.
