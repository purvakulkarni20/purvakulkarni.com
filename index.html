About

I turn complex problems into products people actually want to use.

As a SAFe® AI Product Manager and Implementation Solutions Consultant, I have built my career at the intersection of business complexity and user need, translating complex requirements into products that deliver meaningful outcomes. At UKG, I lead 40+ enterprise implementations simultaneously, defining requirements, closing capability gaps, and shaping the roadmap for one of the largest Human Capital Management platforms in the world.

Then I decided to build something of my own.

Meal Melody is an AI-powered app that eliminates meal decision fatigue. Users scan their pantry or fridge, and the app surfaces recipes they can cook from what is already there, across 50+ global cuisines, with a personal AI chef guiding them step by step. I took it from concept to launch solo, authoring the PRDs, designing the UX, shipping AI-powered recipe personalization with multilingual and voice capabilities, and leveraging feedback from 20+ beta users to drive a 45% increase in daily active usage.

I am drawn to companies where product is taken seriously, where teams move with intention and urgency, and where the work delivers measurable value to the people who use it.

What I Bring to the Table

  • Product thinking grounded in problem definition, solution design, and outcomes
  • Defining product requirements that guide focused feature development
  • Driving continuous value delivery through user insight and rigorous prioritization
  • Structured tradeoff decision-making across competing product constraints
  • Building scalable, user-centric products within enterprise value streams
  • Data-informed product judgment across the full lifecycle
